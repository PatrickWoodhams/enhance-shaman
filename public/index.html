Three actions that move the needle quickly, without creating a new mistake loop.

Stabilize your swings

Get into sync, then bias toward main hand first. This improves Flurry charge efficiency and keeps Windfury value where you want it.

2

Pick the variant that solves your constraint

Sub Elemental is about shock tempo and fire value. Sub Restoration is about hit, mana efficiency, and coverage. Choose based on what is failing, not what looks exciting.

Compare sub specs
3

Add one weaving system at a time

Twisting and weaving are optional power. Add them only after your baseline is stable, and stop if the conditions are not there.
